ORM Marketing

Orm Marketing

It is essential to know what customers are saying about your business and how to respond to their comments. At the operational level, we use robust ORM tools such as Tagboard, SocialMention, Google alerts, etc. to to identify keywords and hashtags related to your organization.

As soon as your brand is mentioned online, we are notified instantly. We ensure that positive mentions are amplified and the negative mentions are responded to immediately. If you're unsure about how to manage your brand's online reputation using the best ORM tools and techniques, Auctech Marcom can help.
